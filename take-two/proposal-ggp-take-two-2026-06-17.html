Partnership Proposal: GGP × Take-Two Interactive

Renew the baseline, expand with the Directory · 12 Months · July 2026 to July 2027
To: Alan Lewis, Chair, Take-Two Charitable Giving Committee
From: Gordon Bellamy, CEO, Gay Gaming Professionals; Professor of the Practice, USC
Read-ahead for: the next standing EXCELLENCE cadence meeting
This proposal has two parts. First, renew the baseline that already works, now as clear, separate line items. Second, expand with the Directory, our highest-priority new asset. The recommended Option B does both, inside the budget envelope you already operate in.

Part 1 · The Baseline (renew)

RENEW · SEPARATE LINE ITEMS
  • GGP Scholars — $30,000. Dedicated support for the flagship cohort, seven years running in GGP’s 20th year. Take-Two recognized as a GGP Scholars supporter.
  • CYSTEM Scholars — $25,000. Take-Two as a valued co-funder of the CYSTEM graduate cohort — top-performing graduate students from USC, CMU, and NYU, each paired one-on-one with an industry mentor. CYSTEM is supported alongside the Bigglesworth and Houser foundations; this is a co-funding role, not sole sponsorship.
  • 2K Studio Tour at GDC — in-kind. Take-Two continues to cover the transportation and coordination that bring the GGP and CYSTEM scholars to the tour each year, a tradition Alan personally joins. Credited here, not a cash ask.

The baseline is $55,000 in direct support ($30K GGP Scholars + $25K CYSTEM), plus the 2K Studio Tour that Take-Two already makes possible. This is the floor, and it grows the relationship rather than stepping back from it.

Part 2 · The Expansion (priority)

NEW · HIGHEST PRIORITY
  • The Directory — $40,000. The US Video Game Education and Scholars Pipeline, a board-ready map of the field from elementary through graduate, with AI readiness as a first-class category and a “where dollars go furthest” tag on every entry. Launches at gaygamingpros.org/directory this summer with Take-Two as named launch partner. Funds the public launch, Year-1 maintenance, a donor-facing dashboard, an AI-readiness overlay, and verified outcomes for the top programs. (A starter directory today; the build-out expands and verifies coverage.)
  • AI Readiness Track — $5,000. An “AI in the Studio” module added to CYSTEM, 1 to 2 office hours led by Take-Two engineers, and an “AI in the Studio” segment inside the 2K Studio Tour at GDC 2027.

With Take-Two as launch partner, the Directory stops being our cohort’s resource and becomes the field’s infrastructure, a public good with your name on the front door, across both GDC and USC.
