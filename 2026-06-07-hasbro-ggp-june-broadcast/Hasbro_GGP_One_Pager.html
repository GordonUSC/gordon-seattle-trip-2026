About GGP

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit, twenty years in service. Universal excellence, particular lens. Our lens — LGBTQ+ professionals and students in the games industry — positions us to serve and center underestimated communities. We are not niche. Eric Francksen, Executive Producer, lives in Wisconsin and hosts the Dragon House.

Grateful to be in our third year of partnership with Wizards of the Coast. Hasbro CEO Chris Cocks and his son joined our community at Zero Proof 2025.
