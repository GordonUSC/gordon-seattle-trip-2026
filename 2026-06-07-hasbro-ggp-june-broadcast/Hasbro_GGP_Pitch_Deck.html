08   Zero Proof · case study GGP × Hasbro Marketing XP · June 2026
The example topic, detailed

The event that redefined community activation at GDC.

What made the partnership work
  • Authentic alignment. WotC's brand, built on imagination, adventure, and diverse heroes, genuinely reflects the GGP community.
  • Audience you cannot buy. GGP brings a credentialed, discerning audience. A community with trust and history.
  • Shared stage presence. When Mark Purvis and John Hight took the stage alongside Gordon and Eric, that was not a logo placement. It was a public commitment.
  • Sober-inclusive design. Zero Proof was not a gimmick. It was intentional design that expanded who could be fully present.

09   What this means GGP × Hasbro Marketing XP · June 2026
Takeaways from Zero Proof for your marketers

Four lessons your marketers carry into Monday.

01
Community trust is a distribution channel.
GGP gave WotC access to 600+ credentialed gaming professionals with a single activation. Communities like GGP can move your brand faster and deeper than paid reach alone.
02
Brand alignment is a product decision.
The partnership worked because WotC's IP values (inclusion, imagination, heroism) matched GGP's community values. Hasbro brands have enormous latent alignment with diverse gaming communities.
03
Inclusive design is marketing strategy.
Zero Proof was not just an event choice. It was a statement that expanded the tent. Designing for inclusion in activations creates advocates, not just attendees.
04
Live events create relationship infrastructure.
The GDC event did not end at GDC. It opened doors to ongoing collaboration. The best activations live beyond the moment and grow into durable partnerships.
