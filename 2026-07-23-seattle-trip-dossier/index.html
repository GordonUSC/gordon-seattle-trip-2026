The places

🏔️ Leavenworth, WA

Bavarian village in the Cascades · ~2.5 hrs east of Seattle via US-2

A former railroad-and-logging town remade in the 1960s as a Bavarian alpine village — timbered facades, biergartens, the Wenatchee River, mountains on every side. Draws ~2 million visitors a year. Front Street is the walkable German-themed core. Your base for the wedding weekend.

~85°Late-Jul high~52°Overnight low8:48pSunset 7/25

Warm and dry (driest month). Highs/lows are climate normals, not a dated forecast. Bring a layer for the evening reception.

💒 Mountain Springs Lodge · Ponderosa Meadow

The wedding venue · ~25–40 min from downtown Leavenworth (Plain, WA)

Destination wedding property in Beaver Valley (Plain, WA), meadows, ponds, Aspen groves and towering Ponderosa Pines, up to 250 guests, with onsite cabins. Ceremony spaces: Ponderosa Meadow (yours, pond + Cascade view), Aspengrove, and the Beaver Creek Barn. After-party at the ponderosa house. Elevation ~1,850–1,900 ft.

🍺 München Haus

Tonight's dinner · 709 Front St, Leavenworth · daily 11a–10p

Bavarian grill + open-air beer garden, est. 2001. Order at the counter: sausages charbroiled outdoors on artisan buns, 20+ condiments and house mustards, their famous apple cider kraut, local Icicle Brewing beers on tap. Pet-friendly, no reservations. Recommended to you today.

Hours are aggregator-sourced, worth a quick same-week check.
