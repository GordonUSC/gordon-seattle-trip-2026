## What this proposal is

The Directory is ready. The **US Video Game Education and Scholars Pipeline** is a board-ready map of the field, from elementary through graduate, with **AI readiness** as a first-class category and **"where dollars go furthest"** tags on every entry. It launches at **gaygamingpros.org/directory** this summer. With Take-Two as the named launch partner, the Directory becomes the structural anchor of games-education infrastructure for everyone, not just our cohort. **That is the spine of this year's partnership renewal.** Everything else (CYSTEM, the 2K Studio Tour, the Scholars Breakfast, the new AI Readiness track) continues underneath it.

## Why now

Six years of Take-Two partnership, **every year since 2019, the same year our GGP Scholars program launched**, with the CYSTEM Mentorship Program (2023), the annual 2K Studio Tour at GDC (a tradition you personally participate in), and the GGP Scholars Breakfast at GDC 2026 that Christopher Kim coordinated. Our most consistent corporate partnership in twenty years of GGP, and the partner who has been with us since the Scholars program existed. **Pride 2026 is in cycle this month**, AI readiness has become the threshold question for talent attraction in games, and the rising generation is graduating into the hardest entry-level market in two decades. **The Directory is how Take-Two anchors the field at the exact moment the field needs an anchor.**

## Option A · Directory Launch · $40,000
**12-month engagement · launches summer 2026**

The Directory exists in draft. Option A funds the public launch and Year-1 maintenance as a Take-Two-anchored resource.

- Public web launch of the US Video Game Education and Scholars Pipeline at **gaygamingpros.org/directory**
- Filterable and searchable interface (age, region, cost, audience served, AI-readiness)
- **"Powered by Take-Two Interactive, launch partner"** attribution prominently displayed
- 200+ programs at launch with verified scope data; quarterly refresh for 12 months
- Annual usage report with downloadable cuts for funders and educators
- Press cycle at Pride 2026 launch: GamesBeat, PocketGamer.biz, Polygon, EdSurge

**What we own:** build, hosting, editorial standards, quarterly refresh, press
**What Take-Two owns:** launch-partner brand approvals, launch comms participation

**Honest assessment:** Option A is the structural anchor. It positions Take-Two as the partner who made navigation of the games-education field possible. It does *not* extend CYSTEM, the Studio Tour, the Breakfast, or fund AI readiness curriculum. Several pieces of value are left on the table.

## Option B · Directory + Pipeline + AI ⭐ · $100,000
### Our recommended choice

**12-month engagement · spans Pride 2026 to GDC 2027 to Pride 2027**

Option B includes the Directory at launch scale, continues every existing tradition, and adds the AI readiness layer. **At $100K, this is the best year of the partnership in dollar terms while still inside the budget envelope you already operate in.** Six-year cumulative T2 history: $162,500. $100K in one year is a credible stretch from there, anchored on the existing relationship.

### B.1 · Directory Launch + Year-1 Maintenance ($50,000)
Everything in Option A, plus:
- Donor-facing dashboard: gap maps, cost-per-outcome, "where dollars go furthest" surfaced as a funder lens
- Verified outcomes data for the top 50 programs (research procurement is the work this funds)
- AI-readiness overlay tagging every entry
- Expanded launch scope to ~250 programs with deeper data

### B.2 · Named Take-Two CYSTEM Cohort ($30,000)
A 12-month cohort of **10 scholars**. **CYSTEM Year 4** under Alex Rogers's continued leadership. Take-Two named on the cohort; scholarship dollars stacked from the existing CYSTEM funder mix to reach full per-scholar amounts.

- $2,500 Take-Two anchor portion per scholar × 10 = $25,000 (stacks with CYSTEM co-funders)
- $5,000 cohort coordination, GDC travel support, mentor onboarding
- 1-on-1 ERG mentor pairings (each scholar matched with a Take-Two ERG member)
- 6 mentor sessions over the year, GGP-curated

### B.3 · 2K Studio Tour + Scholars Breakfast at GDC 2027 ($15,000)
- Underwrites the **2K Studio Tour** for the cohort and the **GGP Scholars Breakfast** in San Francisco
- Both are existing traditions Alan personally values and participates in
- Take-Two named as industry sponsor in press and our annual report

### B.4 · AI Readiness Track ($5,000)
- "AI in the Studio" curriculum module added to CYSTEM
- 1 to 2 AI fluency office hours led by Take-Two engineers
- "AI in the Studio" segment within the 2K Studio Tour at GDC 2027

**Outcomes (12 months):**
- Directory live by Pride 2026; 5,000+ unique users in year 1; 250+ programs at launch; quarterly refresh on schedule
- 1,500+ press impressions across the Pride 2026 launch cycle
- 10 scholars deployed; 80% mentor pairing completion; **at least 3 cohort members in industry roles by July 2027**
- 45+ scholars at the 2K Tour and Breakfast at GDC 2027
- 100% of cohort completes the AI module; at least 3 case-study reflections published
